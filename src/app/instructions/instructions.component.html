Warning

a. Individuals with any of the below mentioned conditions cannot participate in the challenge:

  • Pregnant women
  • Recent Surgery
  • Severe Cardiac Condition
  • Kidney Failure
  • Cancer
  • Severe Arthritis (or any Bone deformity)

b. Rules and Regulations of the Challenge:

  • Assessment form and Diagnostic report are compulsory
  • The challenge for the following week will be shared with you every saturday. On accepting the challenge, the weekly challenge should be started every monday.
  • Weight should be shared with us every Saturday.
  • Every week’s challenge gets carry forward to the following weeks. So on week 6, one should be following challenges of week 1 to week 6.
  • The weight should be measured on the same weighing machine every time, at the same time of the day (empty stomach).
  • Failure to submission of the details requested will lead to the participant’s disqualification.
  • Individuals with highest fitness and weightloss will win exciting prizes towards the end of the challenge.
